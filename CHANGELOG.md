# Changelog

## v1.0 — 2026-07-04

Initial public release: 28 modules over about two years.

- Spine: Imperial College mathematics + Stanford CS109 probability → CS229 with required problem sets → CS230 → CS224N or CS231n specialization (five modules each, ending at LoRA/evals/agents or CLIP/SAM-lineage/multimodal) → capstone with Docker, CI, messy-data, leakage-check, uncertainty, and model-card requirements
- Written completion gates with spaced retrieval on every phase; correctness checks (gradient checks, reference implementations, built-in assignment sanity checks) on every from-scratch build
- Lecture and assignment references pinned to named offerings (CS229 Autumn 2018 playlist, CS224N Winter 2026, CS231n 2025) with match-by-topic drift caveats
- FAQ, per-module progress tracker, issue templates, weekly link-check CI, and an annual pin-review maintenance list

Before public release the curriculum went through a series of structured review passes: pedagogy, factual accuracy, beginner experience, internal consistency, Masters-path alignment, 2026–2028 currency, assessment design, repo health, global accessibility, and a final holistic check.
